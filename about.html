Our Story

Founded in 2026 by Furqan Khatti, IndusRoboTix was born out of a passion for making robotics accessible to the Pakistani maker community.

"Our mission is to bridge the gap between high-end industrial robotics and local student innovators by providing precision-engineered, affordable kits."

Why IndusRoboTix?

  • Local Sourcing: Reduced lead times and no international shipping hassles.
  • Precision: Every acrylic piece is custom-designed for a perfect fit.
  • Support: Direct access to technical support in your timezone and language.
